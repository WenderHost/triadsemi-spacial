Circuit Details

  • Family of projected capacitive touchscreen controllers
  • Ability to rapidly and cost effectively create touchscreen controllers optimized for a given screen size
  • Combined drivers and receivers in a single ASIC for smaller displays
  • A two chip solution of a low-voltage driver IC and a receiver IC for larger displays
  • A daisy-chain-able high-voltage driver IC and low-voltage receiver IC for the largest display sizes
  • Precision analog front end (AFE)
  • Data converters
  • Serial communications like with daisy-chaining

IP Blocks Utlized

  • Cap-touch low voltage driver tile
  • Cap-touch high voltage driver tile
  • Cap-touch low voltage receiver tile
  • Band Pass Filter
  • Analog to Digital Conversion
  • Combined Analog and Digital Automatic Gain Control (AGC)
  • Bandgap, Precision Reference Network
  • SPI, custom daisy-chaining link
