Focused Agile ASIC™

A Focused Agile ASIC contains a mix of agile and ‘hardened’ circuits. Typically, digital portions of the design are implemented as standard-cell logic while some or all of the analog is implemented with ViaOnly reconfigurable agile circuits.

Agile ASIC™

An Agile ASIC is 100% ViaOnly reconfigurable. Any of the analog or digital circuits can be reconfigured by making a single via mask layer change. Focused and Agile ASICs help get products to market a full year ahead of traditional methods.
