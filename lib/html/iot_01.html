This consumer market custom IC was used for remote monitoring and control of precision sensors and actuators in an Internet of Things (IoT) application. The application had very high production volumes. The design was realized as a full-custom mixed signal ASIC.

Why the Customer Chose Triad as their IC Design and Production Partner

  • High-volume opportunity, Tight schedule, Steep production ramp.
  • The customer solicited proposals from all the major custom IC manufacturers.
  • The customer had a strong desire for a partner that combined excellent design skills
  • Also, high-volume production expertise was a key selection criteria.

The customer selected Triad as their ideal partner for designing and manufacturing this custom IC.
